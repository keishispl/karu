1.2.0 - Mining Made Easier!

Buying the lumberjack scroll from custom crafting table costs 8 stacks of any log and 1 sugar

Buying the chunkmine scroll from custom crafting table costs 6 stacks of stone/deepslate (or cobble variant), 8 iron ingot and 1 sugar

Clicking the lumberjack scroll onto an axe will transfer that enchantment(?) to that axe

Clicking the chunkmine scroll onto a pickaxe will transfer that enchantment(?) to that pickaxe

Mining while shifting with an axe with lumberjack will attempt to mine out the whole tree

Mining while shifting with a pickaxe with chunkmine will mine out a 3x3 of that block (Only stone variants, deepslate, nether blocks and ores work)

1.1.0 - The Hunting Update

Hoglins drop lard instead of leather

Placing an amethyst cluster above a crafting table will give you access to the custom crafting table

Added knife (beheading 1), beheading dust & toolbox to the custom crafting table

Clicking a beheading dust on a sword or axe will upgrade its beheading (Max is level 3)

The higher the beheading enchantment, the higher the chance you will get warden shard (from wardens) or player heads (from players)

Toolbox will be almost empty at first, but clicking toolstations onto the toolbox will unlock that specific item inside the toolbox

Lard can be crafted into candles

1.1.1

Elytra Boosting with fireworks is disabled

Using Grappling Hook while gliding will cost 60 mana instead of the usual 30

Regenerating mana while gliding will be 2 mana/0.75s instead of the usual 2 mana/0.25s

For creating a beheading dust in the custom crafting table, the 5x golden apples will be replaced with 4x diamond

Added discord connection to the minecraft server

1.1.2 - Mailing

Added mails

You can craft mails by putting paper in a crafting table

Right clicking the mail will prompt you to edit mail content

Left clicking the mail will cancel the action

Sending text in chat while editing mail will change its content

Mail can be cleared by shift right clicking it while editing mail content
