Items

Custom Items for Unique Uses

Swim Speed

When you join, you will receive the dolphin's grace effect.

It'll make you swim a lot faster.

Resurrection

When you die, this ability will activate. The next time you die it won't happen and then it'll reset.

When the ability activates, you will get revived like a totem of undying.

Vampire

When you hurt someone, this ability has a 25% chance to activate.

When the ability activates, 25% of the amount of damage your opponent took will be given will be given to heal you.
