Items

Custom Items for Unique Uses




Vanilla Texture

Grappling Hook

30 MANA per USE

Buy it at the Fletching Table for 16 sugar, 3 string & 2 sugar

This item will push you forwards when you reel in. Works well with Elytra as a transportation tool.




Vanilla Texture

Teleportation Wand

10 MANA per USE

Buy it at the Fletching Table for 8 obsidian, 2 eye of ender & 1 stick

This item will teleport 8 blocks in front of you if there is no blocks there.

Anti-Podzol Staff

70 MANA per USE

Buy it at the Fletching Table for 16 podzol, 16 grass block & 2 stick

Replaces nearby podzol with grass blocks.




Vanilla Texture

Snowball Cannon

2 second cooldown

Buy it at the Fletching Table EXTRAS for 16 snowball, 2 iron ingot & 2 sugar

This item will launch a snowball. Which will freeze & damage nearby players when it lands.




Vanilla Texture

Flamethrower

20 uses | 10 second cooldown

Buy it at the Fletching Table EXTRAS for 1 flint and steel, 1 iron ingot & 2 blaze rod

This item will launch fire. Which will set players on fire. If overheated, it will ignite you as well.



Brimstone

5 second charge

Buy it at the Fletching Table EXTRAS for 8 golden apple, 1 iron ingot, 1 tripwire hook, 2 string & 3 stick

This item will shoot a laser that deals 5 hearts of damage. In return, you will take damage as well.
